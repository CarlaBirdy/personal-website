Australian Land Conservation Alliance Conference #PLC2022

The Australian Land Conservation Alliance is a member organisation made up of people and organisations that contribute to private land conservation. I attended the virtual PLC2022 organised by ALCA on the 8 to 10 March. I presented in the ‘NSW Research Showcase’ and spoke about my work at UQ on research funded by the NSW Koala Research Strategy.

You can read more about it here: https://alca.org.au/alca_event/plc2022/
